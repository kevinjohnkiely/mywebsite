ABOUT ME

I am a self-motivated, creative and reliable person with over a decades experience in designing websites and graphics. I enjoy the challenge of gathering requirements on a project, sketching out the details with low and high fidelity wireframes, and seeing that through the design phase to completion. I enjoy other creative areas such as Arts & Crafts which I feel enhance my overall design capabilities.

I have a keen eye for detail and am eager to advance a career through the I.T./Design sector. While my skillset is listed below, I am a firm believer in on-going learning and upskilling, so am currently learning JavaScript frameworks, such as React.js and modern ES6+ JavaScript. Some of my small projects can be viewed on my Github account page.

Recently I have built a project using React.js and Bootstrap front end framework, a "Movie Match" app where the user can log in and find other users with similar taste in movies. The back-end database is hosted with Google Firebase. I really enjoyed working through this project and taking my development skills to the next level. The app is live here: https://movie-match-4fe2b.web.app/ and the code is hosted in my Github account under "React Movie Match"
